📄 PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT
Project: Personal Portfolio (AI Engineer – Showcase Style)
1. Overview

Objective:
Design and develop a single-page, high-performance portfolio website that presents an AI Engineer with strong capabilities in system design, machine learning, and real-world AI deployment.

Primary Goals:

Impress recruiters within 60 seconds
Convert freelance visitors into clients
Showcase technical depth and systems thinking
Maintain a futuristic, smooth, and interactive experience
2. Target Audience
Primary:
Recruiters (AI / ML / Data roles)
Hiring managers
Secondary:
Freelance clients (AI solutions, automation, backend systems)
3. Design Philosophy

Style:
Controlled Futurism (clean base + intelligent interactions)

Keywords:

Minimal but dynamic
Futuristic but professional
Interactive but not distracting
